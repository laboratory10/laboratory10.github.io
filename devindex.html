About Laboratory10

Laboratory10 is a small team that is passionate about science and space exploration. Accomplishing this goal would allow more people to benefit from research data and inspire the next generation of explorers. MarsLink

MarsLink was built with the goal to give people an easy way to stay in touch with their inner scientist as Curiosity studies Gale Crater on Mars.

MarsLink allows real-time Mars images from Curiosity to be used as the background for your device. When active, MarsLink will cycle through that dayâ€™s downloaded images every half hour. An auto-update feature can be enabled to automatically check for new images from the rover every 24 hours. With both of these options active, you never need to open the app to see the latest images from Mars.
